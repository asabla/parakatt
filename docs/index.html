How it works

Three seconds from thought to text

No apps to switch. No windows to open. Just your voice.

01

Hold the hotkey

Press Option+Space from anywhere on your Mac. A subtle overlay confirms you're recording.

02

Speak naturally

Talk at your normal pace. Parakeet STT runs on Apple Silicon with sentence-level timestamps.

03

Release to paste

Let go and your transcription appears in the active text field. Optionally cleaned up by a local LLM.

Features

Built for privacy and speed

Everything runs locally. No accounts, no API keys required.

Fully on-device

NVIDIA Parakeet runs through ONNX Runtime on your Mac. Audio never leaves your machine.

Meeting transcription

Dual audio capture grabs both microphone and system audio for full meeting transcripts.

LLM post-processing

Optionally clean grammar, reformat, or restructure via Ollama, LM Studio, or OpenAI.

Sentence timestamps

Navigate long transcriptions with a visual timeline. Every sentence is anchored in time.

Custom dictionary

Regex-based rules to auto-correct names, jargon, and acronyms that trip up the model.

Full-text search

FTS5-powered search across your entire transcription history. Find anything you've ever said.

Rust + Swift

Compute-heavy STT in Rust via UniFFI. Native macOS UI in SwiftUI. Best of both worlds.

Zero telemetry

No analytics, no tracking, no phone-home. Not even crash reports. Your data stays yours.

Works offline

Once the model is downloaded, everything works without internet. Airplane mode friendly.
